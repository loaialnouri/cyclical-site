# Cyclical

A cycling computer built from the ground up for iPhone.

**Privacy & Support:** [loaialnouri.github.io/cyclical-site](https://loaialnouri.github.io/cyclical-site/)

## The Problem

Dedicated bike computers cost anywhere from $200 to $700 — and you're still stuck with a tiny screen, clunky firmware updates, and limited customisation. Meanwhile, the cycling apps available on your phone today were either designed in the early 2000s and never meaningfully updated, or they focus on social features and route planning while treating the live ride experience as an afterthought. None of them offer the same level of sensor integration, real-time data, and visual polish that riders expect from a modern device.

You already carry an incredibly powerful computer in your pocket. It has GPS, Bluetooth, a beautiful display, and enough processing power to run a space mission. Why should you need a second device strapped to your handlebars?

## What Cyclical Does Differently

Cyclical is purpose-built for cyclists who want a proper bike computer without the cost or compromise. It turns your iPhone into a full-featured cycling head unit with capabilities that rival — and in many ways exceed — dedicated hardware.

**Customisable dashboard** — A widget-style tile grid that you arrange to show the metrics that matter to your ride. Speed, power, heart rate, cadence, elevation, distance — drag, resize, and organise them however you like. No fixed layouts, no one-size-fits-all screens.

**Full BLE sensor support** — Connect heart rate monitors, power meters, speed and cadence sensors, and Garmin Varia-compatible rear radar. The same Bluetooth accessories that work with your Wahoo or Garmin work with Cyclical.

**Turn-by-turn navigation** — Import GPX routes from any source and get clear, real-time navigation guidance with distance to next turn, elevation profiles, and route progress — all while your live metrics stay visible.

**Ride analytics** — Post-ride breakdowns with speed splits, heart rate zones, power data, elevation profiles, and detailed maps. Understand your performance without exporting to another platform.

**Apple ecosystem integration** — Apple Watch heart rate, Apple Health workout logging, and Strava upload built in. Your data flows where you want it.

**Designed for iOS 26** — Built entirely in SwiftUI with Apple's Liquid Glass design language. This isn't an Android port or a cross-platform wrapper. It looks and feels like it belongs on your iPhone.

## Privacy First

No accounts. No analytics. No ads. No server-side storage. Your ride data stays on your device. The full privacy policy is available on the [support site](https://loaialnouri.github.io/cyclical-site/).

## Built By a Cyclist

Cyclical was born out of frustration. After spending years cycling with overpriced dedicated computers and underwhelming phone apps, the answer became obvious: build something better. Every feature exists because a rider needed it on the road — not because a product manager added it to a roadmap.
